About Zurich Together

Zurich Together is an international social media community that brings together people from diverse backgrounds in and around the Zurich area.

Our aim is to create opportunities for socializing, connecting, and making new friends through a range of events. Whether you're an expat or a local Swiss resident, you're welcome to join our community and be a part of creating a vibrant and inclusive social scene in Zurich.

Come be a part of Zurich Together and let's make new memories together!
